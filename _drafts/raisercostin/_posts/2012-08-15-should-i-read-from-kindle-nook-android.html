--- layout: post title: Should I read from kindle, nook, android tablet or ipad? date: '2012-08-15T06:32:00.000-07:00' author: raisercostin grigore tags: modified_time: '2012-08-15T06:38:34.212-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2765432647477607238.post-648984734969941671 blogger_orig_url: http://blog2.raisercostin.org/2012/08/should-i-read-from-kindle-nook-android.html --- The last 3 years I was blocked on an important decision: Should I read from kindle, nook, android tablet or ipad? One week ago I decided I will buy them all and I will see what I will actually use. For unused devices... I have enough relatives to "marry" them.


After several days of investigations I learned that
  1. Nook can't be easily bought in europe. I found in the end a shop in netherland.
  2. kindle can't be easily bought in europe except if you live in UK or Germany. Otherwise you will get it from US by paying a lot of import taxes or you will pay around 50% more.
  3. till i will buy a kindle I bought a book to be seen in Kindle For PC. It's awful, I can't imagine how that can look better on a kindle. After 2 hours i managed to convert it to a pdf since I don't plan to read it from the Kindle for PC. It looks the same but at least you have mobility and a faster reader.

The best devices that can be used for reading are
  1. Best kindle in this moment is the KindleTouch - has e-ink, any other one can't compete with other tablets
  2. Best nook is "Nook Simple Touch with GlowLight" - glow light removes one big drawback of e-ink devices
  3. Best android tablet - Nexus 7
  4. Best ipad - new ipad naturally, but not significantly better than ipad2

Conclusions
  1. I decided that I will not buy any more books in a format that is not already pdf. Probably influenced by the previous readings http://www.codinghorror.com/blog/2012/04/books-bits-vs-atoms.html, http://www.codinghorror.com/blog/2012/07/coding-horror-the-book.html
  2. I don't want to buy more dead-tree books as well since:
    1. I can’t carry them all with me
    2. I need to wait till they are delivered
    3. I need to pay for delivery
    4. most of the money don’t go to the writer but to the publishing house which already destroyed the book design.
  3. I will buy drm free pdfs or with my name inside them (as the ones delivered by www.manning.com).
  4. The display to be used for them.... maybe the nook, maybe a future eink kindle, always my pc, very often my future ipad and android, current android phones.
  5. The best option to have the pdfs on all these devices: google drive and/or dropbox.
